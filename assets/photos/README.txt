Place character photos here to texture the 3D models.

File naming (must match character ID):
  vessel.png          (or .jpg)
  honored_one.png
  ten_shadows.png
  hakari.png
  perfection.png
  strongest_of_history.png
  boss_sukuna.png
  boss_gojo.png
  boss_mahoraga.png

Photo tips:
  - Front-facing portrait works best (face maps to head mesh)
  - Square or near-square aspect ratio preferred
  - 256x256 to 512x512 resolution recommended
  - PNG with transparency or JPG both supported
  - The photo will be mapped onto the head and torso
